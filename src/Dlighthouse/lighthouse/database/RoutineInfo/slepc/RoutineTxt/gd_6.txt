Generalized Davidson (GD) [Morgan and Scott, 1986] introduces the first expansion that uses an arbitrary preconditioner, which is applied to the residual to try to enrich the approximation in the direction of the desired eigenvector.

REFERENCE

Morgan, R. B. and D. S. Scott (1986). Generalizations of Davidson's method for computing eigenvalues of sparse symmetric matrices. SIAM J. Sci. Statist. Comput., 7(3):817-825.
