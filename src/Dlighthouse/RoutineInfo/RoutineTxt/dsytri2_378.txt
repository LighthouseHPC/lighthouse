  <dl class="section user"><dt>Purpose: </dt><dd><pre class="fragment"> DSYTRI2 computes the inverse of a DOUBLE PRECISION hermitian indefinite matrix A using the factorization A = U*D*U**T or A = L*D*L**T computed by DSYTRF.
